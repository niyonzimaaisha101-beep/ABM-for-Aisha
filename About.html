Our Story

American-Burundi Mall (ABM) is a premier shopping destination located in the heart of the city. Established in 2020, ABM has quickly become a favorite spot for both locals and tourists alike. Our mall features over 200 stores, a variety of dining options, and entertainment facilities to ensure a fun and enjoyable experience for all visitors.

Our Mission

At American-Burundi Mall, we are committed to providing an exceptional shopping experience that combines quality products with outstanding service. We strive to create a welcoming environment where customers can find everything they need and enjoy their time with us.

Our Vision

We envision American-Burundi Mall as the leading retail destination in the region, known for its diverse offerings, innovative services, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Our goal is to continuously evolve and enhance our offerings to meet the changing needs of our community.
